## Color Schemes

### AI & Automation Frontier (Blue Theme)
- **Primary**: RGB(0, 168, 232) - Bright Blue
- **Secondary**: RGB(14, 165, 233) - Sky Blue
- **Use**: Headers, bullets, decorative elements

### Digital Marketing & Reputation (Green Theme)

## Typography Hierarchy

```
TITLE (Cover Page)
├─ 24pt, Bold, Navy
└─ Center aligned

SUBTITLE (Cover Page)
├─ 14pt, Normal, Dark Gray
└─ Center aligned

SECTION HEADERS
├─ 16pt, Bold, White
├─ Colored background box
└─ Left aligned with padding

BODY TEXT
├─ 10pt, Normal, Text Gray
├─ Justified or left aligned
└─ 5mm line spacing

BULLET POINTS
├─ 10pt, Normal, Text Gray
├─ Colored dot (matches theme)
├─ 8mm indent
└─ 5mm line spacing

FOOTER TEXT
├─ 7pt, Italic, Dark Gray
└─ Center aligned
```

## Spacing & Layout

- **Page Margins**: 20-25mm all sides
- **Section Spacing**: 10mm between sections
- **Paragraph Spacing**: 7mm between paragraphs
- **Line Height**: 5mm for body text
- **Header Height**: 12mm colored box
- **Bullet Indent**: 8mm from left margin
