MRECAI Website Developer Implementation Plan

This document provides a complete, detailed breakdown of all requested website changes, improvements, and integrations 
for MRE Consulting & Insurance (MRECAI). It is designed for the development team to use as an actionable project 
blueprint to ensure full alignment with company branding, functionality goals, and user experience expectations.

1. Overview

The MRECAI website must present a professional, modern, and interactive digital presence reflecting the company’s 
dual focus on consulting and insurance services. All branding must reference the company as either “MRECAI” or 
“MRE Consulting & Insurance.” No other variants should appear anywhere on the website, meta titles, or schema.

2. Homepage Updates
•	2.1 Header and Branding

- Increase the logo size in the top-left corner (desktop: 48–60px height; mobile: 36–44px).
- Remove the business hours section from the top-right.
- Ensure “24/7 Service Support Available” appears consistently site-wide.
- All logo versions must link to the homepage.

•	2.2 Hero Section (Top Stats)

- Current stat boxes (Clients Served, Rating, Years, Success Rate) appear clickable but have no functionality.
- Developer must either make them non-clickable or link each to a relevant page or scroll anchor.
  • Clients Served → About Page
  • Rating → Reviews Page
  • Years of Experience → Company History Timeline
  • Success Rate → Testimonials or Proven Track Record Section

•	2.3 'Why Choose MRECAI' Section

- Update section title to “Why Choose MRE Consulting & Insurance.”
- Each of the six icons/buttons must be clickable with hover effects and route to the appropriate page or section:
  • Expert Team → About Us
  • Fast Results → Services Overview
  • Innovative Solutions → AI & Automation Services
  • Personalized Service → Intake or Consultation Page
  • Proven Track Record → Reviews/Case Studies
  • Trusted Partner → Strategic Partners Section

•	2.4 Service Buttons

- Change all service buttons to MRECAI blue (brand color).
- Add 3–4 bullet points per service card summarizing benefits.
- Maintain consistent hover and click states throughout the website.

3. Page Structure & Navigation

The About section should be restructured into three dedicated subpages to improve navigation and SEO clarity.

•	3.1 About the Company
• Include mission statement, history, and company values with a timeline starting from 2024.
•	3.2 About the Founder
• Create a dedicated biography page for Matthew Epstein with photo, credentials, and philosophy.
•	3.3 Strategic Partners

• Create a partner showcase with carrier, financial, accounting, and legal partners.
• Each partner should include logo, name, description, and website link.

4. Chatbot & Automation Integrations
•	4.1 Chatbot

- Replace or debug the existing chatbot to integrate with:
  • MRECAI intake system (Google Forms or CRM)
  • Email acknowledgment automation
  • Knowledge base FAQs
- Chatbot must:
  • Respond within 3 seconds
  • Offer guided options (e.g., Get a Quote, Book a Call)
  • Pass data directly into CRM or lead database

•	4.2 Booking & Contact Automation

- Integrate Calendly for consultation scheduling.
- Create an Intake Form page linked to all insurance and client onboarding forms.
- Embed live reviews directly from Google Business, Yelp, and Facebook.

5. Contact & Social Media Integrations

- Contact form must send messages to Matthew@mrecai.com.
- Include clickable phone number for mobile users.
- Add social icons linking to all official company pages:
  • Google Business
  • Yelp
  • Facebook
  • LinkedIn
  • Instagram
  • YouTube
- Include mailing list opt-in with confirmation automation.

6. UI / UX Enhancements

- Improve font sizes and contrast ratios for readability.
- Add brand-colored accents for CTA buttons and hover effects.
- Optimize layouts for mobile and tablet users.
- Ensure consistent vertical spacing between sections.
- Enable fixed navigation and scroll anchors for better usability.
- Add subtle scroll and fade animations on content load.

7. Content & Branding Consistency

- Only “MRECAI” or “MRE Consulting & Insurance” may be used site-wide.
- Update any old or inconsistent references.
- Final content will be filled post-design sprint; all placeholders should be prepared for future copy insertion.

8. Pre-Launch Quality Assurance Checklist

Before launch, confirm the following:
- ✅ All links and buttons route correctly.
- ✅ Chatbot functions with CRM intake and email automation.
- ✅ Calendly and contact forms tested for desktop and mobile.
- ✅ Google/Yelp/Facebook review feeds display correctly.
- ✅ Page load speed under 3 seconds.
- ✅ SSL and responsive design confirmed.
- ✅ Accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 AA) met.
